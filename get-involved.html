Get Involved

Get involved.

Three ways to engage. Email hello@undosatech.com.

One

Researchers with archived cohorts.

If you have completed vision-science or neuroscience imaging cohorts sitting on a lab server, and you would like to discuss whether they might be responsibly resurrected as part of a reuse infrastructure, this is the conversation UndosaTech would most benefit from now. No commitment is being asked for — only a conversation.

Two

Prospective clinical or academic co-leads.

A clinical or scientific co-lead with standing in UK vision science or adjacent neuroscience would materially change what this project is capable of. If that could be you, please make contact.

Three

Funders and grant reviewers.

If you work with a funder whose remit covers UK research-data infrastructure or vision research, and you would be willing to offer feedback on the shape of an application before it is submitted, that would be a valuable form of engagement.

How to make contact

One email address, checked by the founder.
