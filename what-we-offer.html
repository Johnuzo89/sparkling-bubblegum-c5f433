What We Offer

A platform where the analysis comes to the data — and every result can be verified.

These capabilities are built and running on the platform today. Not a roadmap — the working system.

Five things you can do the day you're onboarded

Each is enforced by the software itself — access control, disclosure control, and audit are mechanisms, not promises in a policy document.

Find archived data

A governed catalogue of reactivated ophthalmic and neuro imaging cohorts, with provenance and access terms attached to every dataset.

Train without moving it

Federated learning runs the model inside each institution. Only model updates ever leave the building — never the images, never the records.

Get privacy-safe answers

Differentially private queries, synthetic datasets, and small-cell suppression on every output — the numbers you take away can't be reverse-engineered back to a person.

Prove where it came from

Every result is issued a signed Verifiable Research Certificate that binds it to its data lineage, its disclosure settings, and a tamper-evident audit trail.

Hand a regulator the evidence

One click generates a governance evidence pack for the UK, EU, or US framework — assembled from live platform evidence, not written by hand.

Discover & access

A catalogue for data that had gone dark.

Most research imaging stops being usable the moment its original study ends — not because it's gone, but because nobody can find it, understand it, or get permission to use it. UndosaTech turns those archives into a browsable catalogue with provenance and access terms on every entry.

  • Governed data catalogue — cohorts described, versioned, and searchable, each carrying its own lineage.
  • Structured access requests — request, review, and approval handled in-platform, with the decision written to the audit trail.
  • Institution-scoped isolation — row-level security keeps every institution's holdings walled off unless access is explicitly granted.
Analyse without moving data

The model travels. The data stays home.

Instead of copying sensitive imaging into a central pool, UndosaTech sends the computation to each institution and brings back only what's safe to share. This is the same federated approach that has matched roughly 99% of centralised performance across multi-site imaging studies — without the data ever leaving its custodian.

  • Outbound-only federated nodes — an institution runs a node that reaches out; nothing reaches in. The container image is cryptographically signed and ships with a software bill of materials.
  • Trusted Research Environment workspace — an in-platform, controlled space to work, aligned with the Five Safes and SATRE.
  • Foundation-model fine-tuning — adapt modern imaging foundation models to a cohort while the backbone stays frozen, so historical scans yield signal that wasn't extractable when they were captured.

Data never centralised

Images, records, and audit logs remain in UK-resident infrastructure by default. Cross-jurisdiction access requires explicit permission, and the platform records it.

Privacy-preserving outputs

Take away the answer, not the patient.

Every result that leaves the platform passes through disclosure control. You get research-grade findings; the underlying individuals stay unidentifiable.

  • Differentially private queries — calibrated noise with a per-dataset privacy budget that the platform enforces and spends down transparently, so a dataset can't be quietly interrogated to exhaustion.
  • Synthetic data generation — statistically faithful stand-in datasets for prototyping and teaching, with no real records inside.
  • Statistical disclosure control — small-cell suppression applied automatically to every table and export before it is shown.
  • Governed SQL console — an analytics sandbox for aggregate queries, locked to disclosure-safe results.
Prove it — to anyone

A result you can verify, not just trust.

Screenshots of an internal dashboard prove nothing to a journal, an ethics committee, or a funder. UndosaTech issues cryptographic proof instead — and anyone can check it without an account and without contacting us.

  • Verifiable Research Certificates — each output is signed (Ed25519) and bound to its data lineage, its disclosure settings, and the audit-trail state at the moment it was produced.
  • Tamper-evident audit trail — a hash-chained log where any alteration to a past entry breaks the chain and is detectable.
  • Provenance graph — a full lineage from dataset to study to model to output, so a claim can always be traced to its source.
  • Offline, public verification — certificates verify against a published key. The proof outlives the platform.

Independently checkable by design

Verification requires only the certificate and the public key — not access to our systems. That's the difference between "we say so" and "check for yourself."

Interoperate & comply

Speaks the standards your data already uses.

Where an accepted standard exists, UndosaTech adopts it rather than inventing an alternative — so the platform fits into existing research and clinical data infrastructure instead of fighting it.

  • OMOP CDM v5.4 with the medical-imaging extension — the common data model for observational research.
  • FHIR R4 ingestion — clinical records mapped from FHIR bundles into that common model.
  • Regulator-ready evidence packs — governance evidence assembled for the UK (Five Safes · DSPT), EU (GDPR · EHDS), and US (HIPAA · Common Rule) frameworks on demand.
  • Reactivation Index — a federated view of which institutions hold what, built from aggregate, disclosure-safe profiles the nodes compute locally.
